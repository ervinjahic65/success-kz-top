Frequently Asked Questions

What is {Company} ?
{Company} is a cloud-based eCommerce platform that plugs directly into Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, giving you real-time data synchronisation for products, pricing, inventory and orders.
How does the integration with Dynamics 365 Business Central work?
The solution installs a lightweight extension inside Business Central that exposes secure APIs. consumes those APIs in real time—no batch jobs or manual exports—so anything your team updates in BC appears instantly online.
Can I run B2B and B2C portals at the same time?
Yes. You can launch separate B2B and B2C storefronts from the same back-office, each with its own catalogues, pricing rules, checkout flows and theme, all connected to a single Business Central company.
Do you support multiple languages and currencies?
Absolutely. detects the visitor’s locale and can display any language configured in Business Central. Currency presentation follows your BC currency table and includes automatic FX rate updates if enabled.
Is onboarding or training included?
Every subscription comes with a dedicated implementation specialist who guides you through setup, theme customisation and data mapping. We also provide video training and a knowledge-base for future hires.
What are the subscription options?
We offer monthly and annual plans. Pricing scales with order volume and portal count, and there is no per-user fee. Contact sales for a tailored quote after your free demo.
How long does a typical implementation take?
A standard rollout takes 4–6 weeks from kickoff to go-live, depending on catalog size and design complexity. Complex B2B workflows may extend timelines slightly.
